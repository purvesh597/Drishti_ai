// Capabilities

AI that speaks the road

👁️
Real-Time Detection

YOLOv8n runs at inference time of 2.4ms per frame, detecting traffic signs, obstacles, and hazards with 91.9% mAP50 accuracy.

🔊
Voice Announcements

Every detected sign triggers an instant spoken alert — "Speed Limit 60 ahead", "Red Light detected" — keeping users informed without a screen.

📖
OCR Text Reading

PaddleOCR reads shop signs, labels, and text in the scene. Results are spoken aloud with regional language support including Hindi & Marathi.

🌐
Multilingual Support

Regional language voice output — English, Hindi, Marathi — making it accessible to India's diverse population, a key differentiator.

⚡
Multi-Threaded Pipeline

Parallel threads for capture, YOLO inference, OCR, and voice — ensuring no blocking and smooth real-time performance on edge devices.

📡
WebSocket Streaming

FastAPI + WebSocket backend streams detection results in real-time to any connected device — phone, earpiece, or wearable.
